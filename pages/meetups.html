Meetups


Thank you for stopping by to learn more about our meet-ups and classes. At our meet-ups we begin with a short introductory lecture on getting started with the Arduino to ensure that complete beginners can get started with confidence. We have a partnership with Programmng Electronics Academy to grant free access to their Arduino Course For Absolute Beginners while at our meetups. Learning how to program electronics has never been easier.

More experienced users are free to start working at their own pace through the course modules of Arduino step by step, with over 20 hours of content in 110 lectures, this course will take you from zero experience in electronics or programming to Arduino Master. This course is designed for anyone interested in learning electronic design and C programming. No experience is required.
