 Keypad, Part 1: Introduction
In this presentation, I will demonstrate how to connect a common 4x4 membrane keypad that contains numbers, letters “A”, “B”, “C”, and “D”, a “*” and a “#”. I will do it in two ways. First, I'll use the keypad library that comes with the Arduino IDE in a sketch that reads the keypad, while the keypad is connected in parallel. In demo 2 I will show you how to hack together a solution that allows a connection that uses a single signal pin.
